Designing an Icon

This project was my first to involve design thinking structured into mulitple iterative steps and lots of critical, creative thinking. We were tasked to design an app icon that was reflective of a function based on a subject prompt given to every single student. In my case, I had to design an app icon that “intentionally gets you lost”. The primary objective of this assignment was to focus on integrating design principles into an icon that conveyed the function of the application.

Step 1

MINDMAPPING

The first step in the iterative process was to sketch out anything related to my app concept. As someone who was (and is) very new to the process of design thinking, I wrote down anything that expressed emotions, objects and simple notions that related to the key words of my concept. I had to ensure that the symbolic meaning of the icons were reflective of the intentions within the app function.
